Creator-led IRL is the most familiar first bridge from digital trust to funded real-world participation.

The stakeholder version: the Sponsor starts with someone people already trust, Analog turns that trust into a governed activation, attendees get a better reason to show up, and the result becomes proof for whether the Sponsor should fund more.

## When To Use This

Use this play when:

- the Sponsor already trusts a creator, affiliate, ambassador, customer, or community
- a CreatorCommerce or partner deployment exposed a strong creator/affiliate roster
- the creator has product love or category authority
- the activation can become a real community moment, not a shallow meet-and-greet
- the Sponsor wants content, attendance, trial, category growth, or proof

## Setup

1. Define the Sponsor objective.
2. Pick the creator or community cohort.
3. Write the community angle: what people are showing up to do together.
4. Create or choose a campaign.
5. Create an event set if this is part of a broader creator/community lane.
6. Accept or create the Host/collab.
7. Create the Host event.
8. Configure the hosted event page if RSVP, tickets, claims, or attendee capture matter.
9. Attach sponsor budget and spend card.
10. Confirm proof lanes before money is used.

## Brief Checklist

Include:

- Sponsor goal
- Host or creator role
- event concept
- attendee benefit
- approved spend categories
- content expectations
- disclosure expectations when the creator receives value
- proof requirements
- renewal or unlock condition
- hosted page, claim, import, or audience proof plan
- files/assets the creator needs
- asset request or shipment needs

## Good Examples

- cookware sponsor funds a creator-hosted cooking class
- running brand funds a creator-led recovery morning
- app sponsor funds a founder breakfast with a trusted community operator
- wellness brand funds a creator x run club collaboration

## Bad Examples

- sponsor sends product with no budget, proof, or host incentive
- creator appearance has no community angle
- spend cannot be watched
- host has no repeat path
- the event exists only for content extraction

## CreatorCommerce Handoff

If the lead came from CreatorCommerce, capture:

- creator or affiliate relationship tier
- product/category affinity
- sales or persuasion evidence
- city/audience fit
- IRL willingness
- offer or affiliate mechanics
- conversion data readiness

Then use [CreatorCommerce Demand Bridge](/strategy/creatorcommerce-demand-bridge) to decide whether the next action should be creator-led IRL, creator attendance, VIP/customer night, or local market-entry activation.
