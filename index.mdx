## What This Is

These are founding operating docs, not generic product docs. They explain how Analog makes community-led IRL fundable, measurable, and repeatable without flattening gatherings into ads or one-off event services.

Analog starts Sponsors-first because capital is the unlock. The company currently builds around three Sponsor perspectives: eComm Brands that sell physical products, Tech Companies that sell digital products to strategic users or accounts, and Public/Non-profit Funds that underwrite public-good experiences and outcomes.

## Narrative Spine

Every page should ladder back to the same spine:

```text
Digital gets abundant
-> real-world trust becomes scarcer and more valuable
-> Sponsors need safe ways to deploy IRL/community capital
-> Hosts need cash, services, tools, and proof to build better gatherings
-> Analog governs the spend and captures what happened
-> proof decides the next dollar
-> product memory becomes an IRL culture graph
```

If a page does not clarify market demand, Sponsor capital, Host supply, spend governance, proof, renewal, product memory, or execution, it probably does not belong in the live docs yet.

The company has five motions:

1. **Owned city dogfood**: AnalogNYC uses Analog to run IRL, categorize spend, capture proof, and build supply.
2. **Cash-funded playbooks**: Sponsors and public funders use micro grants, macro grants, controlled budgets, and services/tools to help Hosts accomplish real community goals.
3. **Agency engagements**: brands buy access to Analog's human network before the marketplace is liquid.
4. **Market formation**: city leads and owned IRL create sponsor-ready supply.
5. **Media, connectors, and funding**: stories make the gathering economy legible and route builders into governed funds.

The MVP centers on Sponsors, Hosts, Campaigns, Event Sets, Collabs, Host Events, spend cards, hosted pages, files/docs, outreach, tasks, shipments, and sponsor-visible proof.

The first clean business logic is cash-based Host enablement. Movement communities are one useful proof case, but the larger strategy is to fund trusted Hosts across many community families, then use proof, services, media, and renewal logic to decide who gets the next dollar.

## The Pipeline We Are Building

Every sponsor target should map to a sponsor perspective, market trigger, brief, supply ask, pilot, and proof standard. Use [Sponsor Personas And Pipeline](/strategy/sponsor-personas) for the working map.

## What To Believe

```text
Communities make markets. Brands monetize those markets.
```

Analog exists because the next great growth channel will not be another dashboard, feed, or ad surface. It will be the real-world rooms, routes, rituals, spaces, clubs, and local operators where trust is formed before it becomes data.

Analog is the financial layer that lets Sponsor capital reach those communities with taste, governance, proof, shared spend, AI-monitored reporting, social growth, sentiment, and renewal logic.
