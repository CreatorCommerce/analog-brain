These docs are designed for fast operator use. Start with the smallest page that frames the job, then pull canonical references only when you need product facts.

## If You Are Planning A Pilot

1. Read [Current Thesis](/strategy/current-thesis).
2. Use [12-Month Market Focus](/strategy/twelve-month-market-focus) to decide whether the pilot swims with an existing stream or creates a repeatable causeway.
3. Use [Lean GTM Operating Model](/strategy/lean-gtm-operating-model) to keep the pilot scoped to what a sub-9-person team can actually operate.
4. Pick the relevant sponsor perspective in [Sponsor Personas](/strategy/sponsor-personas).
5. Use [MVP Use Cases](/strategy/mvp-use-cases) to choose what the pilot should test.
6. Use [Audience Graph And Proof](/strategy/audience-graph-and-proof) if the pilot depends on RSVP, claims, imports, or attribution.
7. Score the market with [Testing Grid](/market/testing-grid).

## If You Are Running GTM

1. Read [12-Month Market Focus](/strategy/twelve-month-market-focus).
2. Read [Lean GTM Operating Model](/strategy/lean-gtm-operating-model).
3. Pick the sponsor perspective in [Sponsor Personas](/strategy/sponsor-personas).
4. Use [Early City Staffing](/strategy/early-city-staffing) to decide which city lead or contractor bench should support the motion.
5. Use [Supply Motion](/market/supply-motion) and [Event Prospecting Catalog](/market/event-prospecting-catalog) to turn sponsor demand into host-side options.

## If You Are Activating Supply

1. Read [Host Models And Personas](/strategy/host-models-personas).
2. Use [12-Month Market Focus](/strategy/twelve-month-market-focus) to separate broad opportunity from the causeways Analog should pursue now.
3. Use [Lean GTM Operating Model](/strategy/lean-gtm-operating-model) to keep city asks sponsor-backed and operationally realistic.
4. Read [Dot Connectors As Rockstars](/strategy/dot-connectors-as-rockstars) when the supply motion depends on local trust, taste, and community builders.
5. Read [Prestige Media And Funding Platform](/strategy/prestige-media-funding-platform) when the motion needs editorial status and on-platform fund access.
6. Read [Early City Staffing](/strategy/early-city-staffing) when deciding who should lead NYC, LA, Miami, or SF.
7. Decide whether you are accessing existing supply or creating new supply with [Supply Motion](/market/supply-motion).
8. Use [Event Prospecting Catalog](/market/event-prospecting-catalog) when supply exists but needs to be found, enriched, claimed, or prospected.
9. Use [City Leads And Host Unlocks](/market/city-leads-host-unlocks) when a local operator can create more options.
10. Use [Media And Owned IRL Engine](/strategy/media-owned-irl-engine) when city leads should turn field discovery into social, newsletter, print, video, and owned gatherings.
11. Use [Asset Need Intelligence](/market/asset-need-intelligence) when blockers repeat.
